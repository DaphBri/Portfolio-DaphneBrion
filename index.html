About

Daphné Brion
Front end developer, but not only ...

Hi. My name is Daphné Brion.

Last year I decided to fulfil my dream of becoming a Front-End Developer and took action by following an intensive 7 month training at "Bruxelles Formation" (CEPEGRA). Now I am ready to start a 2 month internship starting half of may.

Who am I? Well. I am a trilingual mother living in Brussels, with over 18 years’ experience as production and graphic employee at "Wolters Kluwer Belgium". Throughout the first part of my career I focused on graphic design and desktop publishing. The second part up to last year is characterized by working with data- and image processing within a xml content CMS.

Outside the professional bubble I am also passionate about art and more specific about photography. I love spending time with my family, to travel and experience adventurous activities.

I am looking forward to start my internship with people that share my love for web-design and photography.

Thank you for taking the time to consider my candidacy and I look forward to hearing from you in the near future.
